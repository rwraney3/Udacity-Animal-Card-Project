Labrador Retriever

Labs are America's favorite breed of dog

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Origin: St. John's Water dog (extinct)
  • Top Speed: 12 mph
  • Life Span: Approximately 12 years

Labs are misnamed - they are not from Labadore, but from NewFoundland. Originally, they were used for fishing. Which may explain why they love the water. Pure labs come in three colors, chocolate, black and yellow.
